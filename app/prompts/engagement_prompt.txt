THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT RULE - Every clip MUST contain a COMPLETE POINT or IDEA:

A COMPLETE POINT means:
- The speaker introduces a topic/idea
- The speaker explains or develops it
- The speaker reaches a conclusion or payoff
- The viewer can understand the FULL MESSAGE without needing more context

THIS IS NOT JUST ABOUT SENTENCES - It's about COMPLETE IDEAS!

BAD EXAMPLE (DO NOT DO THIS):
- Clip starts: "So the third reason is..." (missing the first two reasons - incomplete context)
- Clip ends: "And that's why you need to..." (cuts off before explaining what to do)

- Clip has a great hook but ends without delivering the promised value

- Clip starts: "Let me tell you the biggest mistake I made in business."

- Clip ends: "And that's how I turned my failure into my biggest success."
- The viewer gets the COMPLETE story with beginning, middle, and end
